Health Tips

Diabetes

  • Maintain a balanced diet with low-sugar options.
  • Incorporate regular physical activity into your routine.
  • Monitor your blood sugar levels regularly.

Heart Health

  • Follow a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Limit saturated fats, salt, and processed foods.
  • Exercise regularly to strengthen your heart.

General Wellness

  • Stay hydrated by drinking at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  • Get enough sleep to help your body recharge.
  • Practice stress management techniques like meditation or yoga.
